# 🎮 Buckshot Bob

I am developing this minigame for my first game jam. Buckshot Bob is a 2D rogue-like where you play as Bob, 
a guy in a space suit being swarmed by [to decide what to call enemies - aliens] and all you have is your trusty shotgun.

When you kill an **enemy**, the enemy will split into 2 **smaller enemies**, but you can also shoot an extra **shotgun shell** for each time
you split an **enemy**. whenever you get damaged by an enemy, you lose a **shotgun shell**. The core gameplay loop is powering up your shotgun 
to being able to fire an insanely high amount of shells an avoiding being overrun by enemies and losing your last shotgun shell - i.e. certain death.
